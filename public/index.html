posterior
(anatomy) Nearer the rear or hind end; nearer the caudal end of the body in quadrupeds or the dorsal end in bipeds.
(statistics) A type of conditional probability that results from updating the prior probability with information summarized by the likelihood via an application of Bayes' rule.
